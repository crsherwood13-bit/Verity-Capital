5-Touch Cold Email Sequence Funnel 01 — Primary Outbound
D1
Subject: "The deal that got away"
Data point opener. No pitch. One observation, one question. "Is deal origination still a bottleneck for [firm]?" No CTA to book. Goal: open rate test.
D3
Subject: "Re: The deal that got away"
Follow-up. Share the mechanism: "800+ private owners/day." Link to cold LP or brief. Goal: drive to cold LP.
D7
Subject: "What 9 qualified conversations in 84 days looks like"
Case study. Contractor mandate. 1,400 owners, 9 convos, 84 days. Specific numbers. Goal: social proof, reply trigger.
D14
Subject: "Quick question about your current mandate"
Binary question: "Running off-market origination or primarily brokered?" Easy yes/no. Creates conversation without pressure. Goal: reply bait.
D21
Subject: "Closing the loop — [First Name]"
Permission close. "I'll stop reaching out after this." Link to cold LP. "We only take mandates where we can deliver." Goal: future re-engagement, no burn.
