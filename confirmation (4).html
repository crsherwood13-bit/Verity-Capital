Mandate Checklist

What to have
ready for the call.

You don't need all of this — but the more you have, the more specific and useful our first conversation will be.

Your target sector and geography. Specific vertical and region, not broad categories. "Commercial HVAC in the Mountain West" not "B2B services."

Financial parameters. Your target EBITDA range and revenue range. Deal size floor and ceiling.

Capital status. Whether you have committed capital, a fund structure, or are operating as a fundless sponsor. This shapes the mandate narrative.

Your timeline. When do you want to be in qualified conversations? Are you ready to transact now or building pipeline for 6-12 months out?

What you've already tried. Any outreach, broker relationships, or origination attempts you've run — even if they didn't work. Context helps us scope the engagement.

Your pitch deck or mandate overview. Optional but useful. Even a rough one-pager gives us the narrative foundation faster.
